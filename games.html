GAMES

Keansburg Amusement Park offers a variety of fun games for all ages. From classic carnival games like ring toss and balloon darts to modern arcade challenges, there’s something for everyone. Test your skills, win prizes, and enjoy the excitement. It's the perfect spot for friendly competition and family fun!

BALLOON POP

Kneasburg Amusement Park features a fun and classic balloon pop game in its carnival zone. Guests can test their aim by popping colorful balloons with darts to win exciting prizes. The game is perfect for all ages and adds a nostalgic carnival vibe to the park. It's a must-try attraction for anyone visiting the park!

WHACK A MOLE

Kneasburg Amusement Park offers a classic Whack-a-Mole game for all ages to enjoy. Grab a mallet and hit the moles as they pop up, testing your speed and reflexes. It's a fun, fast-paced challenge with exciting prizes for top players. A must-try for some nostalgic amusement park fun!

BASKETBALL

Kneasburg Amusement Park features an exciting basketball toss game in its carnival zone. Show off your shooting skills by sinking hoops to win awesome prizes. Whether you're a seasoned player or just trying your luck, it’s a fun challenge for all. Step up and see if you’ve got what it takes to score big!

FISH BALL TOSS

Kneasburg Amusement Park’s fish bowl toss game is a timeless favorite in the carnival area. Test your aim by tossing ping pong balls into tiny fish bowls to win fun prizes. It's a game of skill and a little luck, perfect for players of all ages. Give it a try and see if you can land the winning shot!
