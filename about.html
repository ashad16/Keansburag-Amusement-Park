About Us

Welcome to Keansburg Amusement Park, a timeless destination for family fun and unforgettable memories on the Jersey Shore! For over a century, we’ve been entertaining visitors with thrilling rides, exciting games, and delicious boardwalk treats. Whether you’re seeking adrenaline-pumping roller coasters, classic carnival games, or a relaxing seaside escape, we’ve got it all.

From young adventurers to seasoned thrill-seekers, Keansburg Amusement Park is your ultimate destination for fun, laughter, and joy. Join us and make memories that will last a lifetime!


Park Rules and Regulations

1.
Respect all guests and park staff to maintain a friendly and enjoyable atmosphere.
2.
Smoking is permitted only in designated areas within the park.
3.
Outside food, beverages, and alcohol are not allowed on park premises.
4.
Follow all height and safety restrictions for rides to ensure your safety.
5.
Proper attire, including shirts and footwear, is required at all times.
6.
Children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by a responsible adult.
7.
Dispose of all trash in the designated bins to help keep the park clean.
8.
Unauthorized photography or videography for commercial use is strictly prohibited.
9.
Pets are not allowed, with the exception of service animals.
