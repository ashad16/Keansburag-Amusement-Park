DINNIG & ENTERTAINMENT

​Keansburg Amusement Park offers a variety of dining options and entertainment to enhance your visit.

TOUCAN GRILL

Toucan Grille at Keansburg Amusement Park serves chargrilled burgers, including California-style options. It’s perfect for a quick, tasty bite. Enjoy it while exploring the park!

ENTERTAINING MAGIC SHOW

Keansburg Amusement Park features family-friendly attractions, including the "Magic Ring" ride for kids, and occasionally hosts entertaining magic shows during special events.

ICE CREAM

Keansburg Amusement Park offers exciting rides and attractions, paired with the perfect treat—delicious ice cream to make your visit even sweeter!

LEMONADE DRINK

At Keansburg Amusement Park, you can enjoy exciting rides and quench your thirst with a refreshing lemonade, making your day even more enjoyable!
