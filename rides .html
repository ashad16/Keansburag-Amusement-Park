RIDES

Keansburg Amusement Park features exciting rides for all ages, from thrilling roller coasters to nostalgic carousels. Families love the bumper cars and kid-friendly attractions, while thrill-seekers enjoy heart-pounding adventures. The park perfectly blends classic charm with modern fun. It’s a destination for unforgettable memories!

CAROUSEL

The carousel ride at Keansburg Amusement Park is a classic attraction featuring beautifully crafted animals and chariots. With its gentle spins and cheerful music, it’s a timeless, family-friendly experience that delights riders of all ages!

ROLLER COASTER

The roller coaster at Keansburg Amusement Park offers an exhilarating experience with high-speed twists, thrilling drops, and sharp turns. Perfect for adventure lovers, it’s a must-try ride that guarantees excitement and unforgettable fun!

WATE SLIDE

The water rides at Keansburg Amusement Park offer refreshing fun with splashes, twists, and thrilling drops. Perfect for cooling off on a sunny day, they provide excitement and enjoyment for families and adventure seekers alike!

FERRIS WHEEL

Keansburg Amusement Park's Ferris wheel offers breathtaking views of Raritan Bay and NYC. It shines with colorful lights at night, blending nostalgia and charm. A timeless favorite for all ages!
